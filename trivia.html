🏠 Local Legends

📍 Buddy’s

Located in the Fan at 600 N Sheppard St, Richmond, VA 23221, Buddy’s hosts one of the most lively trivia nights in town. Expect clever questions, cold drinks, and a crowd that’s equal parts competitive and fun. Trivia typically kicks off at 8 PM on Tuesdays.

Vibe: Casual, energetic, and great for groups
